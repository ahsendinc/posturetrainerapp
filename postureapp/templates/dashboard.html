Instructions




    Setup

  1. Wear the Microsoft Band 2 on your left wrist, oriented so you can read the time
  2. Tape one flex sensor to your lower back. The Arduino board can be put in a runners belt. Further setup can be found here.

    3. Squat

  3. Stand with your heels shoulder-width apart. Put your heels under your shoulders and turn your feet out 30 degrees.
  4. Lock your hips and knees.
  5. Squat down. Push your knees out while moving your hips back. Keep your lower back neutral.
  6. Squat down until your hips are below your knees. Thighs parallel to the floor isn’t low enough. You must break parallel.
  7. Squat Up. Break parallel then squat back up. Keep your knees out and chest up. Lock your hips and knees at the top. [1]
