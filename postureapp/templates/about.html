About Posture Trainer



What is Posture
Trainer?

Posture Trainer is an app that helps you improve your form when performing the squat exercise. The app alerts you when your posture is incorrect during a sqat.

How does it work?

Posture Trainer monitors your back posture and alerts if your form or speed is incorrect.

Who should use
Posture Trainer?

Posture Trainer is designed for anyone new to weightlifting! Anyone looking maintain good form when lifting can use Posture Trainer. Even if you have been lifting for a long time Posture Trainer can help remind you to not get sloppy!

I've never been
injured. Why
should I care?

Many studies have shown that weightlifting, either in the gym or in an occupation, can contribute to back problems. Correct posture plays an important role in preventing these injuries. [2,3] It's much easier to prevent injury than to recover from it!

Technology

Posture Trainer incorporates two hardware sensing modalities 1) Arduino Flex Sensor and 2) Microsoft Band 2. This allows Posture Trainer to accurately monitor your back posture and alert you when your form is incorrect.

Sounds great,
what do I need?

  1. Download the PostureTrainer app (Android only, iOS coming soon)
  2. Microsoft Band 2
  3. Flex sensor for Arduino
